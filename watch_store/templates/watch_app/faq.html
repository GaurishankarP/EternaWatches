Frequently Asked Questions

Go to “Add Watch” from the navigation menu, fill in the required details like brand, model, price, and stock, then click Save. The watch will instantly appear in your stock list.

Open the “Manage Inventory” page, locate the watch you want to update, and choose Edit or Delete from the actions column.

Check the Dashboard or Reports section. Low-stock alerts appear in red, and you can generate a “Low Stock Report” anytime.

Only users with Admin privileges can add or edit staff accounts from the User Management page.

Click on the “Forgot Password” link on the login page. If you’re still locked out, contact the system administrator via the Contact page.
