Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Eterna Watches Inventory handles and protects staff data with utmost confidentiality.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information required to manage inventory and user accounts, such as staff names, emails, and activity logs.

2. How We Use Data

The data is used solely for managing inventory records, tracking activity, and sending important system notifications.

3. Data Protection

All information is securely stored with access limited to authorized administrators. Our system uses encryption to protect user credentials and communications.

4. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as system processes evolve. Updates will be communicated to all registered staff and displayed on this page.
