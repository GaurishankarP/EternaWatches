Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Eterna Watches Inventory Management System handles and protects staff data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information required to manage inventory and user accounts, such as staff names, emails, and activity logs.

2. How We Use Data

The data is used solely for managing inventory records, tracking activity, and sending system notifications.

3. Data Protection

All information is stored securely with access restricted to authorized administrators.

We may update this Privacy Policy if system processes change. Updates will be communicated to all staff.
